ReferHub helps you to organize your dental business

ReferHub is a web site dedicated to integrating the general dental practitioner with specialists in order to streamline their communication and bring a better and more timely care to their mutual patients.

About ReferHub

ReferHub is a platform designed by dentists for dentists. Based in MarVista CA, Our focus is to bring digital solutions to the dental industry in areas of communication between dental offices, laboratories, pharmacies, and to the general population.

Digitize Referrals

Tracking

HIPAA compliance

Keep all information backed up on a double server secured cloud back up

Close the Loops

Ensure that all patients referred are connected to the specialist via text, phone, or email so that no treatment plan goes incomplete and no patient falls through the cracks.

What is ReferHub?

Put simply, ReferHub connects the Specialist with the Dentist to collaborate on the patient’s treatment plan, by creating a HIPAA compliant communication channel to transfer information such as Referral forms, Radiographs, final treatment reports, and a convenient chat feature to discuss any and all treatment options.
